Privacy

Data Sovereignty

Commitment

Aether is built on the principle of data sovereignty. We believe your movement and interior data belong to you alone. Our vehicles process neural data on-device, ensuring no sensitive information ever reaches our cloud without explicit consent.

Vehicle Sensors

External cameras and LIDAR arrays are used solely for real-time spatial mapping and collision avoidance. This data is non-persistent and is discarded every 120 seconds unless an incident occurs.

Contact Trace

To request a full copy of your encrypted mobility logs, please authenticate via your Aether ID in the mobile application.
